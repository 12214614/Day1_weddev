Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originates from Hyderabad, India. It is made with basmati rice, marinated meat (usually chicken or mutton), and a blend of spices.[1] The dish is known for its rich taste and fragrant aroma, often garnished with fried onions, fresh coriander, and mint leaves. Hyderabadi Biryani is typically served with raita (yogurt sauce) and salad.
